Central PA Septic Service ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect and use your personal information when you visit our website or use our services in Pennsylvania.

We collect information that identifies or relates to you ("Personal Information"). This includes:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data collected via cookies.

We use your information to:

All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personal Information. We share data only with:

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to analyze web traffic. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, though some site features may not function properly. We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals as there is no uniform industry standard.

6. Data Security and Retention

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to secure your data. We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with Pennsylvania business record-keeping requirements.

You have the right to:

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
