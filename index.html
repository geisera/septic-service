Common questions

Basic answers customers look for before they call.

How often should a septic tank be pumped?

Most homes need pumping every 3 to 5 years, but household size, water use, and tank size can change that schedule.

What are signs my septic system needs service?

Slow drains, sewage odors, wet spots near the drain field, and backups are all signs you should schedule a visit.

Do you handle septic inspections for property sales?

Yes. We offer inspections for buyers, sellers, and real estate transactions, with findings presented in plain language.
